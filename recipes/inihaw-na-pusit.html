Inihaw na Pusit

This inihaw na pusit is wonderful when served fresh off the grill since it is packed with tomatoes and onions and marinated in a sweet and sour vinaigrette. Ideal as a starter or main course.

Grilled jumbo squid is an excellent choice for either an appetising starter or a filling dinner. It's sure to satisfy because it's been grilled over hot coals to juicy perfection, packed with onions and tomatoes, then marinated in a sweet and tangy vinaigrette.
